Toy Wristwatch

Another artifact from the Ipe family, this wristwatch was owned by the daughter of Ammu Ipe. It was found buried in the grass near the History House of Ayemenem. Strangely, instead of telling the time, the wristwatch has the time 1:50 painted on it, as if the owner had wanted the time to always be the same.
